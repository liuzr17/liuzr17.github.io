Liu Zhirui

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Cyber Science and Technology, Sun Yat-sen University(SYSU). I obtained my bachelor's degree from the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University(TJU).

My recent research focuses on AI-Generated Content and low-level vision, with a particular emphasis on the application of diffusion models. Lately, I have been working on a project about relighting 2D images or 3D neural radiance fields.
